Hick's Law

The principle that the time it takes to make a decision increases with the number of choices available.

Organization/Company Name: [Flow Agency]

Website Address: flowagency

Brief Description: The website layout is simple and easy to navigate through. The site also has a simplified menu option to make choices easier to the users.

Rule of Thirds

The principle that an image should be divided into nine equal parts by two equally spaced horizontal lines and two equally spaced vertical lines, and that important compositional elements should be placed along these lines or their intersections.

Organization/Company Name: [Amazon]

Website Address: Amazon

Brief Description:

  • Product Images: The main product image, showcasing the item for sale, is often placed at one of the intersections of these imaginary lines, drawing the viewer's attention.
  • Call-to-Action (CTA) Buttons: CTA buttons like "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now" is positioned along these lines, making them prominent and easily accessible to users.
  • Product Description: Key product details or descriptions might be positioned in a way that aligns with the lines, maintaining a balanced layout and guiding the user's focus.
  • Reviews and Ratings: Customer reviews and ratings is also positioned using the Rule of Thirds to ensure visibility and importance.

    • Contrast

    The principle of using visual elements (such as color, size, and font weight) to make certain elements stand out and draw the viewer's attention.

    Organization/Company Name: [Trainline]

    Website Address: Trainline

    Brief Description:

  • Color Contrast for Text: Trainline use a high contrast ratio between the text and background colors to ensure text is easily readable. For instance, using dark text on a light background.
  • Highlighted Buttons: Important buttons like "Search" or "Book Now" have a high contrast color compared to the background, making them stand out and encouraging users to take action.
  • Icons and Graphics: Icons or graphics used on the Trainline website have high contrast to make them easily distinguishable and enhance the overall visual appeal.
